Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome to Bali – Airport Transfer & Sunset Dinner

Arrive at Denpasar International Airport and be welcomed with fresh frangipani garlands and a cool drink. You'll be escorted to your eco-luxury villa in Ubud. Unwind by your private plunge pool or explore the nearby artisan village. In the evening, join the group for a welcome dinner overlooking lush rice paddies.
